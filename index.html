FAQ

Got
Questions?

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find an answer? Just ask us directly.

Ask Us Anything →

How much does a website cost?

Every project is custom-quoted based on your needs. Most small business websites range from $500–$2,500. We'll give you a clear price before any work begins.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most sites are completed within 2–4 weeks depending on complexity and how quickly content is provided.

Do I need to provide my own content?

You provide the basics (logo, photos, key info) and we handle the rest — copywriting, layout, and design are all included.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every site we build is fully responsive and tested across all major devices and browsers.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes — we offer maintenance plans and are always available to make updates, fix issues, or help you grow the site over time.

What if I already have a website but it's outdated?

We offer redesigns and refreshes. We can rebuild it from scratch or modernize your existing one — whatever makes the most sense.
